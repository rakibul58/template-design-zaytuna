Our Mission

Zaytuna College aims to educate and prepare morally committed professional, intellectual, and spiritual leaders who are grounded in the Islamic scholarly tradition and conversant with the cultural currents and critical ideas shaping modern society.

In 2009, Zaytuna College was established upon a rock solid foundation of classical Islamic knowledge. It was built with the intention of shaping conscientious, committed to studying the Muslim classics. Combining this vigor of form with the best practices in teaching and learning, the College seeks to fulfill its mission to produce God-conscious learners who are conversant in both past and present; employing a traditional liberal arts education.

Liberal arts, in its original sense, meant a rigorous mode of educating body, intellect and appetite; addressing morality, the moral/emotional characteristics (the emotional capacities of the soul (Plato's thumos, epithumia, and logistikon)), an Islamic notion of education rooted in Cosmopolitan Sufi Praxis, as has the George Muslim documented in his seminal work; The Plan of the Odyssey (originally Egyptian, German, and Greek), a variant or inspired by early work by E. A. Havelock.

Zaytuna College seeks to empower elite students through both the BA and MA programs, which are consistent that define both on the Muslim classics and the Euro-American classical tradition that grew from and alongside it. Our aim is to cultivate caring men and women committed to Islam, goodness, and beauty, in the classroom of our Creator and its creation.

The College is dedicated to the proposition that the best of the Islamic intellectual tradition, combined with the best of American higher education, can produce a new generation of leaders equipped with both moral clarity and practical wisdom. Our graduates go on to serve their communities in diverse fields including education, law, medicine, social work, and public service.

The Zaytuna College Campus

Zaytuna College, located in Berkeley, California, is a beacon of Islamic scholarship and learning in the heart of the San Francisco Bay Area. Our historic campus, situated in the vibrant city of Berkeley, offers students access to a rich intellectual tradition while being immersed in one of the most diverse and culturally significant regions in the United States. The campus features classrooms, a library, and prayer spaces, all designed to foster an environment conducive to spiritual and intellectual growth.

Berkeley itself is renowned for its history of free speech, diversity, and innovation. The campus location in downtown Berkeley provides students with easy access to the resources and intellectual opportunities of the larger Bay Area, including world-class libraries, research institutions, and cultural venues. Students are encouraged to engage with the local community and take advantage of the many opportunities for learning, service, and personal growth available in the region.

Our Campus Environment

The campus creates a warm and welcoming environment that encourages intellectual discourse and spiritual reflection. With modern facilities and a supportive community, students find themselves in an ideal setting for academic excellence and personal development.

Located in the heart of Berkeley, our campus provides easy access to public transportation, cultural venues, and the natural beauty of the Bay Area.

A Vibrant Community

We foster diversity, create opportunities for meaningful participation and leadership development, and engage thoughtfully with partners across the Bay Area. Our students are encouraged to take an active role in shaping campus life through various student organizations, cultural events, and community service initiatives.

We believe that higher learning should be characterized by its capacity to nurture the whole person. Our campus community supports intellectual, moral, and spiritual development, creating a vibrant and inclusive environment that welcomes all students committed to academic excellence.
